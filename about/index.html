How We Build Our Team

We are a social enterprise that combines technology and social impact. We use our skills and strategies to create technology solutions that help nonprofits and small and medium-sized businesses (SMBs) build a brand for less, and grow their businesses or causes, while making a positive difference in the world. We work in four simple steps:

  • We find the right partners who share our vision and values, and who need our services to enhance their brand identity and reach their potential.
  • We plan with them on how best we can assist them, and devise a strategy that suits their budget and timeline.
  • We deliver value to them by providing them with affordable and high-quality web app and mobile app design and development, logo design, architectural design, 3D design and model building, cloud migration and AI solution integrations.
  • We give back 15% of our profits to support smaller nonprofits and social initiatives that align with our beliefs, and improve the lives and well-being of the people and communities in Ghana, Nigeria, and the Gambia.

Why Our Team Loves This Mission

We love working with nonprofits and SMBs who need some help with building or improving their technology solutions. We know that many of them may not have the budget or the time to hire qualified professionals to assist them in areas like web app and mobile app design and development, logo design, architectural design, 3D design and model building, cloud migration and AI solution integrations.

Our core mission is to bridge this gap by providing them with affordable and high-quality technology solutions that empower them to grow and achieve their goals. By working with us, they also support other social initiatives, as we donate 15% of our profits to make a positive difference in the world.

We believe this enables entrepreneurs and founders who may not be technically skilled to focus on what they do best - offering their products, services or causing change in society.
