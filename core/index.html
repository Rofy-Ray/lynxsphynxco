What Drives Us

How We Work

We are a social enterprise that combines technology and social impact. We help nonprofits and small and medium-sized businesses (SMBs) build a brand for less, by providing them with high-quality technology and design solutions that empower them to grow and achieve their goals. We work in four simple steps:

  • We find: We identify the right partners who share our vision and values, and who need our services to enhance their brand identity and reach their potential.
  • We plan: We collaborate with our partners to understand their needs, their challenges, and their opportunities, and we devise a strategy that suits their budget and timeline.
  • We deliver: We use our expertise and passion in technology, design, and innovation to create customized and user-friendly solutions that meet the needs and expectations of our partners.
  • We give back: We donate 15% of our profits to support smaller nonprofits and social initiatives that align with our beliefs, and make a positive difference in the world by improving the lives and well-being of the people and communities in Ghana, Nigeria, and the Gambia.

Our Partners

We work with nonprofits and small and medium-sized businesses (SMBs) who need some help with building or improving their brand identity. We understand that many of them may not have the budget or the time to hire qualified professionals to assist them in areas like web app and mobile app design and development, logo design, architectural design, 3D design and model building, cloud migration and AI solution integrations.

Our core mission is to bridge this gap by providing them with affordable and high-quality technology and design solutions that empower them to grow and achieve their goals. By working with us, they also support other social initiatives, as we donate 15% of our profits to make a positive difference in the world.

We believe this enables entrepreneurs and founders who may not be technically skilled to focus on what they do best - offering their products, services or causing change in society.

Our Purpose

We started this business to provide affordable and high-quality technology and design solutions to nonprofits and SMBs, while making social impact by empowering them to grow and achieve their goals.

Our Strategy

We provide the best services to our partners by leveraging our expertise and passion in technology, design, and innovation, to create customized and user-friendly solutions that enhance their brand identity and meet their needs and expectations.

Our Commitment

We are dedicated to giving back 15% of our profits to support smaller nonprofits and social initiatives that align with our beliefs, and make a positive difference in the world by improving the lives and well-being of the people and communities in Ghana, Nigeria, and the Gambia.
